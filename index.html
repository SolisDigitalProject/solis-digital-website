FAQ
Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How quickly can you build my website? +
Most websites are designed, built, and launched within 5–7 business days. Our AI-powered workflow means we move faster than traditional agencies — without sacrificing quality. Complex e-commerce or multi-feature builds may take 2–3 weeks.
What makes Solis different from other agencies? +
We use AI to do in days what traditional agencies take months to deliver — at a fraction of the cost. But every piece of work is refined by humans. You get agency-quality results with startup speed. Plus, we show you exactly what you're losing before we pitch anything.
Do I own my website after it's built? +
Yes, 100%. Once your website is delivered and paid for, you own all the code, content, and design. We'll hand over everything. If you decide to leave, there are no lock-ins or hostage situations with your own website.
What if I'm not happy with the design? +
Every package includes 2 rounds of revisions. We also offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee — if you're not happy, we'll refund you in full. We've never had to use it, but it's there for your peace of mind.
Do you work with businesses outside Leeds? +
Absolutely. While we're based in Leeds, we work with businesses across the UK. Everything is done remotely — video calls, screen shares, and a dedicated project portal. Location is never a barrier.
What's included in the monthly retainer? +
Our Growth and Enterprise plans include ongoing SEO, content updates, performance monitoring, analytics reporting, and priority support. Think of it as having a full-time marketing team — without the full-time cost.
How does the free audit work? +
Enter your email below and we'll analyse your current website's speed, SEO, mobile-friendliness, and conversion potential using our AI tools. You'll receive a detailed report within 24 hours showing exactly where you're losing customers and how to fix it. No obligation, no hard sell.
