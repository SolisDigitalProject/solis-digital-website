## 5. REVISIONS

5.1. The project includes **2 (two) rounds of revisions** after the initial design is presented.

5.2. A "round of revisions" is defined as a single consolidated set of feedback provided in writing (email or shared document).

5.3. Each round of revisions will be implemented within 3 business days of receiving the Client's feedback.

5.4. Additional revisions beyond the included 2 rounds will be charged at **£75 per hour**, quoted in advance.

5.5. Revisions must be requested within 14 days of the initial design presentation. After this period, additional changes will be treated as new work and quoted separately.

## 6. CANCELLATION & TERMINATION

6.1. **Monthly retainer:** Either party may cancel the monthly retainer with **30 days' written notice** sent via email to the other party's registered email address.

6.2. **Before delivery:** If the Client cancels before the website is delivered, a cancellation fee of 50% of the one-off fee will apply to cover work already completed.

6.3. **After delivery:** The one-off fee is non-refundable once the website has been delivered and approved (or after the 14-day revision window has closed, whichever comes first).

6.4. Upon cancellation of the monthly retainer, the Agency will:
- Provide the Client with all website files and a full backup
- Assist with migration to another hosting provider (up to 1 hour of support)
- Remove the Client's site from Agency-managed hosting within 14 days

6.5. The Agency reserves the right to terminate this agreement immediately if the Client engages in illegal activity, misrepresents their business, or uses the services for purposes that could damage the Agency's reputation.
