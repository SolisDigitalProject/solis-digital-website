Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, when performed by qualified medical professionals using approved products, aesthetic treatments have an excellent safety profile. At {{clinic_name}}, all treatments are carried out by trained practitioners in a CQC-registered clinical environment. We conduct a thorough consultation and medical history review before any procedure.
Results vary depending on the treatment. Anti-wrinkle injections typically last 3-6 months, while dermal fillers can last 6-18 months depending on the product and area treated. Skin treatments like chemical peels and microneedling deliver cumulative results with a recommended course of sessions. We'll give you a clear timeline during your consultation.
Most clients describe treatments as mildly uncomfortable rather than painful. We use topical anaesthetic cream for filler treatments to minimise discomfort, and anti-wrinkle injections use very fine needles that most clients tolerate easily. Your comfort is our priority, and we'll talk you through each step of the process.
Most injectable treatments have minimal downtime. You may experience mild redness, swelling, or bruising that typically resolves within 24-72 hours. Anti-wrinkle injections usually have no visible downtime. Skin treatments may involve 1-7 days of peeling or redness depending on the depth of treatment. We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions for your specific procedure.
Pricing depends on the treatment and the amount of product needed. We provide transparent pricing on our treatments page, with final costs confirmed during your consultation based on your personalised treatment plan. We never pressure you into any procedure, and there's no obligation following a consultation.
Your consultation is a relaxed, confidential conversation with your practitioner. We'll discuss your goals and concerns, review your medical history, assess the treatment area, and recommend a personalised plan. You'll receive full information about the procedure, expected results, risks, aftercare, and pricing. There is absolutely no obligation to proceed.
