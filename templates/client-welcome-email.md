## Intake Questionnaire (Google Form Questions)

Create this as a Google Form at forms.google.com. Title: "Solis Digital — Client Intake Questionnaire"

### Questions:

1. **Business name** (Short text, required)

2. **Business full address** (Short text, required)

3. **Contact person's name** (Short text, required)

4. **Contact phone number** (Short text, required)

5. **Contact email** (Short text, required)

6. **What does your business do? Describe your services in 2-3 sentences.** (Paragraph, required)

7. **Who is your ideal customer?** (Paragraph, required)
   *Description: Think about age, location, income level, and what problems they need solved.*

8. **What are your top 3-5 services or products?** (Paragraph, required)
   *Description: List them in order of importance/revenue.*

9. **Do you have an existing logo?** (Multiple choice: Yes / No / Yes but I want a new one)

10. **What are your brand colours?** (Short text, optional)
    *Description: Hex codes if you have them, or describe the colours you'd like.*

11. **List 2-3 competitors or websites you admire** (Paragraph, optional)
    *Description: These help us understand the style and standard you're aiming for.*

12. **What are your main business goals for the next 6 months?** (Paragraph, required)
    *Description: e.g., more enquiries, bookings, foot traffic, online sales*

13. **Do you need any of the following features?** (Checkboxes)
    - Online booking system
    - Contact form
    - Gallery / portfolio
    - E-commerce / online shop
    - Blog
    - Client testimonials section
    - Social media feed integration
    - Live chat / chatbot
    - Newsletter signup
    - Other (please specify)

14. **Social media links** (Paragraph, optional)
    *Description: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X/Twitter, YouTube — paste all that apply.*

15. **Do you have a Google Business Profile?** (Multiple choice: Yes / No / Not sure)

16. **Is there anything else we should know?** (Paragraph, optional)
    *Description: Special requests, deadline pressures, things to avoid, etc.*

### Form Settings:
- Collect email addresses: Yes
- Send confirmation email: Yes
- Limit to 1 response: No (they may need to resubmit)
- Responses go to: Google Sheet linked to your "Solis Digital — Operations" Drive folder
