## 4. Objection Handling

### "We already have a website"

"Yeah, I know - that's actually why I'm calling. We ran an audit on it and the scores suggest it's not performing as well as it could be. For example, your speed score is [X] out of 100 - Google recommends above 90. A slow site means visitors leave before they even see your services. Would it be worth having a quick look at the report together? It's completely free."

### "We can't afford it"

"I totally understand - budgets are tight for everyone right now. Our Starter package is actually just 497 pounds, one-off. No monthly fees, no contracts. And the way we see it, if your website brings in even one or two extra customers, it's paid for itself several times over. Would it help if I walked you through what's included so you can see if it makes sense?"

### "We're not interested"

"That's completely fair, and I appreciate you being straight with me. Can I just ask - are you happy with the number of enquiries you're getting online at the moment? Because the audit we ran flagged a few things that might be affecting that. If nothing else, the report is free and might give you some useful info."

*[If they're still not interested]:* "No problem at all. I'll leave you to it. If anything changes, we're easy to find. Have a good day."

### "We already have someone doing this"

"That's great that you're already investing in your online presence. Out of curiosity, do you know what your current website speed score and SEO score are? The reason I ask is that we audit a lot of businesses in [their industry] and we often find that sites aren't performing as well as the business owner thinks. If you're happy with your current setup, that's brilliant - but if you'd like a second opinion, our audit is completely free and there's no obligation."

### "Send me an email"

"Absolutely, I'll do that right now. What's the best email address for you?"

*[Get their email, then add]:*

"Perfect, I'll send that over today. It'll include your full audit report with your scores. I'll give you a call in a couple of days to see if you had any questions - does that sound alright?"

*[Note: Always follow up. This objection is often a polite brush-off, but the email gives you a reason to call back.]*

### "How do I know this works?"

"Good question. We work with [dental practices / plumbers / estate agents / etc.] across London and Nottingham, and we use AI to build sites that are specifically designed to convert visitors into customers - not just look nice. Everything we build is optimised for Google from day one, and we can usually have a site live in 5 to 7 days. The best way to see if it's right for you is the free strategy call - we'll show you exactly what we'd do and you can decide from there. No pressure at all."
